3. What is Bitcoin ▼

Bitcoin is a form of digital currency that’s created, transferred, and stored electronically. No one controls it, which means it is completely decentralized. Bitcoin uses Blockchain technology, to maintain a highly secure system that requires miniscule transaction fees to transfer value at a revolutionary speed.

4. Why use Bitcoin? ▼

It’s fast, cheap, and secure to use!

Currently, it can take multiple days for a simple transfer of funds to be processed, simply because banks cannot trust that the funds are readily available. With Bitcoin, the Blockchain confirms the transactions and instantaneously transfers the funds for a fraction of the typical fees charged by modern day financial institutions.

10. What is a DAO? (Decentralized Autonomous Org.) ▼

It is an organization that is run by rules programmed through smart contracts. Utilizing Blockchain technology, a DAO provides a secure public ledger that keeps record of financial interactions across the internet. It's protected against forgery by trusted timestamping and the distribution of the public database. This type of system eliminates the need for a third-party facilitator to process the transactions. A prime example of the DAO concept is the Ethereum platform.
